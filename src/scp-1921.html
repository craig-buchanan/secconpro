SCP - 1921

Item #: SCP-1921

Object Class: Keter

WARNING: Containment breach (Possible effect of SCP-173).

Special Containment Procedures: Simply store under plastic alone and separated from other items of clothing.

When not being washed ensure that items left by SCP-1921 are always kept in bright light under recorded video surveillance.

If found bloody it is imperative that it be washed in any commercial washing machine. DO NOT ADD ANY OTHER ITEMS.

Description: SCP-1921 is an anomoly that likes to leave bloodied items of clothing for people to find; typically men's business shirts.

If the finder of said shirt reacts with fear and/or contacts the authorities they almost always die in horrific circumstances within a time frame of a few months.
