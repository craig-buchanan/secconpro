SCP - 814

Item #: SCP-814

Object Class: Euclid

WARNING: Containment breach (Possible effect of SCP-173).

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-814 must be kept in a reflective room with a perpetual bombardment of stroboscopic lighthing.

Personnel entering containment area must wear blinkered glasses to close of peripheral vision and concentrate on keeping their eyes focused on the floor one or two meters in front of them.

Description: SCP-814 is a visual anomaly that appears as analog static or heat blur in one's peripheral vision.

Every 24 hours SCP-814 will leave a nest of egg-like objects on the floor of the containment facility.

It is imperative that these 'eggs' are incinerated as quickly a possible otherwise they can explode releasing a sense of horror and despair to all humans in a two thousand kilometer radius.
